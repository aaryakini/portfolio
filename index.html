Aarya Kini

Hi! My name is Aarya Kini (she/they) and I'm a designer and a journalist, but most of all, a storyteller. I am most passionate about making research-driven work that can impact communities, regardless of their form or scale. My personal practice usually involves researching (in both a journalistic and design sense) real-world topics and exploring them through physical and digital formats, and often a combination of both.

I am currently a dual-degree student at The New School, and I recently graduated with a BFA in Communication Design from Parsons School of Design. In the coming year I will finish up my BA in Journalism + Design from Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts, graduating in May 2025.
