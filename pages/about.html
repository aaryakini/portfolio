Hi! Nice to meet you!

My name is Aarya, and I am in my last semester of a dual-degree at The New School in New York City. I completed my BFA in Communication Design from Parsons School of Design in May 2024, and will graduate with a BA in Journalism in May 2025.

I am currently making things at Partner & Partners, but am also working as a design lab student technician, research assistant, and freelance designer.

When I'm not making fun things at work, I am reading, watching football (the real kind), admiring a sunset, or starting a new documentary. In a previous life, I was also a semi-professional Kathak dancer and currently hold a diploma in the dance form.

I'm always open to new experiences and opportunities. If you like what you see on here, feel free to shoot me an email!
