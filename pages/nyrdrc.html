Typefaces:
FK Raster Roman — Florian Karsten Typefaces

This project involved creating a fictional event for a social campaign that advocates for a meaningful cause. It was divided into two parts — a site specific physical component, and a virtual component to supplement it. Inspired by the recent pandemic of fake news that spread alongside the coronavirus, the cause I chose was ending the spread of misinformation. Hence, centering the conversation on misinformation surrounding COVID-19 and redefining the slogan “Stop the Spread”, I created a series of wild-postings vand a mockup for a website that advocates for media-literacy and self-protection when browsing the web. The visual language for this project was derived from the user-interface of the Mac Lisa.
